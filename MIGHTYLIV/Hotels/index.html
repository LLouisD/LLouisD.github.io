Unfortunately, because of various events on campus, most hotel rooms in Oxford have already been booked through block reservations that weekend. However, any such rooms which remain unclaimed are released a month beforehand; so you may want to check with some of these hotels after March 6th.

As an alternative, we have reserved a block of ten rooms at the Wingate by Wyndham hotel in West Chester, Ohio. This hotel is about 35 minutes from Oxford by car; it is located right off I-75 in a pleasant area with lots of restaurants and amenities nearby. If you make a reservation at this hotel, be sure to mention Miami University to obtain the reduced rate of $89 per night.
