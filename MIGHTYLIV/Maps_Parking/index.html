We have arranged for the university to issue a parking pass which allows you to park for free in any valid place on campus on April 6, but please be aware that this pass does not allow you to park for free at a meter, or in the garage. The parking pass itself also contains a list of standard rules about handicapped spaces, reserved spaces, and yellow curbs.

Click here to download your parking pass.



The recommended place to park is the Withrow lot. To access the Withrow lot, drive north on Patterson past High street, then turn left on Withrow lane (This is easiest to see on the google map at the top of the page). If you follow Withrow lane to the end, you will drive right into Withrow lot. If for some strange reason Withrow lot is full, you should use Pearson lot or North/West Cook Field lot as back-ups.

Click here to see a parking map (note that this map is oriented so north is actually in east's position).



The registration/breakfast will be in the lobby which is between rooms 153/156 and room 165 (see pictures below). We will of course have signs directing you to the right place so don't worry too much about finding your way after you park.

If you find yourself in a building called "Benton Hall," then you are in the building on the left and you have gone too far west.
